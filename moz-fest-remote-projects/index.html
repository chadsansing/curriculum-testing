MozFest Remote Challenge

To build a truly global Web, we need you!

Wherever you are around the world, join the community of Mozillans gathering online and off to celebrate the Open Web! Join the #mozfest stream with @Mozilla and @MozTeach for a weekend of building and making connections across the globe.

Whether you're near or far - traveling to MozFest or kicking around your in hometown - together we can make a more inclusive Web that brings people together regardless of physical distance.

This year, as an onsite or remote MozFest attendee, invite a friend to join you in tackling the MozFest 2015 Remote Challenge.

  1. Send a postcard sharing your work with Mozfest participants near and far - or just say, "Wish You Were Here."
  2. Help us develop curriculum and invite others to test your current project with this 'Test @ the Fest' template.
  3. Remix the animal pictures and noises in Wild about Mozfest to localize the Web and share your part of the world with our global community.
  4. Join the weekend-long @MozTeach tweetchat and interact with live sessions.
  5. Start a conversation on our MozFest Discourse thread.
  6. Share a memory of digital technology with the @mozfesttimeline Twitter account.
