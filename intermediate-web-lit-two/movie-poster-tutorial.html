CSS Fonts: Movie Poster Extension

Something has gone wrong in the movie studio marketing department. A designer has chosen a font for the science-fiction film Bridge to Infinity that just doesn't match the movie's genre and big ideas. Can you use freely available webfonts, like those from Google, to fix this design mistake before the movie's release?

To do so, you'll need to use what you already know about design and webfonts to:

  1. Draw your own conclusions about the movie's genre and big ideas from its title and tagline.
  2. Find the lines of code in index.html and style.css that link to the mistmatched font used on the page right now.
  3. Find a freely available webfont online that fits the movie's genre and big ideas.
  4. Link that webfont to your index.html and style.css files.

Good luck. Drawing on your past work in this CSS module, you can do this!
