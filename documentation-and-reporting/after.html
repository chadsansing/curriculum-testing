After the event, . . .

  • Be timely with the report. Experts suggest creating a report no later than a maximum of 8 days after the event.
  • Write down the key points. Consider three things that worked best, one point that was the most surprising, and two things that you wish tohave to improve for future events.
  • Plan where are you going to add the media and how you are going to share it.
