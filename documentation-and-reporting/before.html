Mozilla Foundation Documentation and Reporting Tips - Before

Use these tips to help prepare for your local Mozilla event.

Before the event, . . .

  • Plan for what kind of information you want to collect.
  • Determine what kind of media you will use.
  • If you are planning to use any online tools like forums or online surveys, be sure to create them before your event.

Regarding tools and apps, make them SIMPLE! You don’t need expensive equipment to report an event. If you have a pro camera it’s cool, but if you don’t, you can useonly need a smartphone.

Finally, make documentation and reporting FUN! Record Vines, Shoot time-lapse, and use any fun application to produce creative media.
