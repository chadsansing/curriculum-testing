Web Literacy Basics II | Section 3. Participating on the Web


Why Do We Use the Web?

Made by Mozilla Learning Networks.

Learners will create and research survey questions about their community's Web use, learning collaborating, community participation, and open practices.

90 minutes to 2 hours

    Post the URL, or Web address, of today's make somewhere highly visible in your room. You may want to post it as a shortened link using a service like bit.ly.
