What if they had the web?

  1. Appoint a judge.
  2. Pick a prompt or tell a story. You might have a deck of cards with prompts, a website that generates them, or judges who tell stories from their own cultures and experiences.
  3. Everyone but the judge should come up with their best web-based solutions to a conflict in each story.
  4. The judge awards a point to the player with the best solution.
  5. Whoever wins the point becomes the next judge.
  6. The game goes on until one player wins five points.

You can remix this page to localize it for your community by changing the names of the stories in its JavaScript file.
