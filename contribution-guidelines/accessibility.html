Contribution Guidelines

Accessibility

Did you alt-tag or caption all media?
Did you create a high-contrast visual design that is easy to read?
Did you tag your work for easy discoverability for your audience?
Did you suggest an appropriate age group?
Did you localize your work for your audience?
