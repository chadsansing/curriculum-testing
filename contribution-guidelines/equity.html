Equity

Did you write instruction and code with a welcoming and inclusive voice?
Did you de-bias your work so it does not discriminate by race, religion, gender (with or without sexual conduct), gender identity, color, national origin, pregnancy, ancestry, domestic partner status, disability, sexual orientation, age, genetic predisposition, marital status, citizenship status, or any other basis covered by applicable laws?
Did you edit to cut out jargon?
